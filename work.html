MyMD - HomeCredit Hackathon 2019
Overview

Joined the Home Credit Hackathon 2019.

Developed a simple mobile app within 24 hours.

The organizer introduced a topic and a problem. Each team must come up with a solution and how to digitalize the Filipinos.

Our app is called MyMD stands for My Medical Doctor. It's goal is to help the patients to book a schedule with their doctors. It's also features a emergency button where the application initiate 911 emergency call or find the nearest the hospital near you.

Our team is composed of 2 Frontend Dev, 2 Backend Dev, and 1 Project Manager.

These were my roles during the hackathon:

  • Backend Dev - Responsible for implementing the business logic and interaction with database
  • Code Reviewer - All code changes(Backend and Frontend) should come through me to ensure the logic are working and correct
  • DevOps - Responsible for maintaining the code repository and deployments

Tech Stacks: PHP, Laravel, Bootstrap, GIT, heroku, and MySQl
